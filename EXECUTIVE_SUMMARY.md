# Executive Summary — Amman Digital Market Analytics

## Top Findings

1. **Consistent Revenue Growth**: The market has demonstrated a steady monthly growth trend in total sales, with visualizations showing a strong upward trajectory in JOD revenue over time.
2. **Capital City Dominance**: Amman serves as the primary engine for sales, contributing a significantly higher share of total revenue compared to Irbid.
3. **Significant Behavioral Variance**: Statistical testing (T-Test) confirmed a significant difference ($p < 0.05$) in average order values between Amman and Irbid, indicating distinct purchasing power across regions.
4. **Category Performance Diversity**: The Grocery category leads the market in terms of total units sold, while the Electronics category shows the highest variance in individual order values.

## Supporting Data

* **Growth Trends**: Supported by `kpi_1_monthly_revenue.png`, which illustrates the temporal evolution of sales performance.
* **Regional Analysis**: Supported by the comparison in `kpi_2_city_revenue.png` and the results of the `city_comparison` statistical test, confirming that the revenue gap is statistically significant.
* **Category Distribution**: Supported by `kpi_3_category_distribution.png` (showing high variance in Electronics) and `kpi_5_category_units.png` (highlighting Grocery's volume leadership).
* **Operational Stability**: The `kpi_4_weekly_trend.png` chart shows that weekly order volume remains stable, suggesting a reliable and consistent customer base.

## Recommendations

1. **Optimize Marketing Spend in Amman**: Since Amman is the primary revenue driver, marketing budgets should be prioritized here to reinforce market dominance and maximize ROI.
2. **Tailored Pricing for Irbid**: Given the statistical difference in purchasing behavior, consider launching economy bundles or localized promotions specifically for the Irbid market to increase its average order value.
3. **Inventory Prioritization for High-Volume Goods**: As the Grocery category leads in unit sales, ensure supply chain resilience for these items to prevent stockouts of high-velocity products.
